Setting up your VIZIO TV is simple and takes only a few minutes. Completing the setup allows you to access SmartCast features, streaming apps, and manage your TV using your VIZIO account.

Follow the steps below to link, register, and activate your VIZIO TV or soundbar smoothly.

Step-by-Step Guide to Set Up Your VIZIO TV
------------------------------------------

1. **Link Your VIZIO TV**

   Sign in to your VIZIO account at **Vizio.com**.  
   Click on **TVs & Soundbars** and select **Add TV**.  
   On your TV, open the **Home Screen**, go to **Extras**, select **VIZIO Account**, and enter the 6-digit code displayed on your screen.

2. **Register Your Product**

   If your VIZIO TV is not already registered, complete the registration process during setup to activate warranty and support services.

3. **Connect Your Device**

   Follow the on-screen instructions to connect your TV or soundbar to a stable Wi-Fi network.  
   You can also create a new VIZIO account during this step if required.

4. **Validate Your PIN**

   When prompted, enter the PIN or validation code shown on your TV to complete the setup process.

Once these steps are complete, your VIZIO device will be fully set up and ready to use.
